Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, properties, and processes.

You can start by browsing our Properties page to see all available listings. Use our Budget Calculator to determine your budget, and then filter properties based on your preferences. You can also contact one of our agents for personalized assistance.

We offer three flexible payment options: Installments for monthly payments, Full Payment for one-time purchase, and Rent for rental properties. You can use our Budget Calculator to explore which option works best for you.

You can book an agent by visiting our Agents page to view all available agents, or use the Book Your Agent option in the Agents dropdown menu. Fill out the contact form with your details and select your preferred agent, and we'll connect you with them.

Commission fees vary by agent and typically range from 2.0% to 2.5% of the property value. When you book an agent through our contact form, you can see the specific commission rate for each agent. All fees are transparent and discussed upfront.

Yes! You can schedule a property viewing by contacting us through our Contact page or by reaching out to one of our agents directly. Simply fill out the contact form with your preferred date and time, and we'll arrange a viewing for you.

Typically, you'll need: proof of identity (government-issued ID), proof of income (pay stubs, tax returns), bank statements, pre-approval letter from a lender (if financing), and any additional documents requested by your agent or lender. Our agents will guide you through the entire documentation process.

The timeline varies depending on financing, inspections, and negotiations. Typically, the process takes 30-60 days from offer acceptance to closing. Cash purchases can be faster (15-30 days), while financed purchases may take longer due to loan processing. Our agents will keep you informed throughout the process.

Yes! We work with trusted lenders and can help you explore financing options. Use our Budget Calculator to estimate monthly payments, and our agents can connect you with mortgage specialists to find the best rates and terms for your situation.

The listed price typically includes the property itself and any fixtures. Additional costs may include: closing costs (2-5% of purchase price), property taxes, home insurance, HOA fees (if applicable), and inspection fees. Our agents will provide a detailed breakdown of all costs during the purchase process.

Absolutely! We have experienced listing agents who specialize in selling properties. Contact us through our Contact page, select "Selling a Property" as your subject, and one of our agents will help you with pricing, staging, marketing, and closing your sale.
